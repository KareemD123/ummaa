Inaugural Event – Save the Date!

We are thrilled to share that the University of Toronto Muslim Alumni Association (UMMAA) has officially launched, and we are hosting our inaugural event on Wednesday evening, November 26th, 2025. This event will be an opportunity to connect with fellow alumni and enjoy an evening of networking, light refreshments, and community building.

• Date: Wednesday, November 26, 2025

• Time: 5:30 PM – 8:30 PM

• Location: By RSVP

• Audience: UofT Alumni and community members

Agenda:

• 5:30 PM – Registration, networking, and light refreshments

• 6:15 PM – Welcome remarks

• 6:30 PM – Key speeches

• 7:15 PM – Networking

• 8:30 PM – Event concludes

RSVP Now

Please RSVP online (spots are limited), and kindly advise any accessibility requirements or dietary restrictions. Please feel free to forward this invitation to others in your network who may be interested.

We truly hope you can join us for this special occasion and look forward to seeing you on November 26th!
