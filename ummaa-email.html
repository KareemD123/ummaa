Our Mission & Vision

The University of Toronto Muslim Alumni Association (UMMAA) connects Muslim graduates from all faculties and generations, fostering a vibrant network rooted in shared faith, academic excellence, and professional growth. We are dedicated to supporting young professionals through mentorship, career guidance, and meaningful opportunities, while cultivating lasting relationships among alumni who share common values and experiences.

UMMAA envisions a thriving community where Muslim alumni actively contribute to the University of Toronto and Muslim Canadian Network through service, knowledge, and social responsibility. We aim to promote Islamic values by empowering alumni to give back, support future generations, and engage in initiatives that reflect professional excellence, community involvement, and interfaith collaboration.

We are committed to building a welcoming environment that celebrates the rich diversity of our Muslim alumni—embracing varied cultural backgrounds, career paths, and perspectives on faith.

Our Story

The University of Toronto Muslim Alumni Association (UMMAA) was born out of a shared vision and a deep sense of community. As former members of the UofT Muslim Students Association (MSA), we experienced firsthand the powerful impact alumni could have on students through mentorship and career networking.

Recognizing the need for a lasting platform to foster these connections, we partnered with the University's Advancement office to establish the University of Toronto's Muslim Alumni Association (UMMAA) - a growing initiative with the potential to become the largest alumni network on campus. On August 12, 2025, UMMAA officially launched as a community hub for connection, support, and growth.

We pray that UMMAA becomes a source of ongoing benefit (ṣadaqah jāriyah) for generations of Muslim and non-Muslim students and alumni alike, inspiring a community rooted in excellence, service, and unity.

Whether you're a student, alumni, or community member, the UMMAAh welcomes you with open arms!
